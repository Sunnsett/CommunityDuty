A.G.Rhodes Health & Rehab's CEO Deke Cateau

At A.G. Rhodes Health & Rehab provides expert and compassionate rehabilitation therapy and residential care to seniors in metro Atlanta.
By volunteering with A.G. Rhodes Health & Rehab, you’ll have the opportunity to give back to your community and the seniors who’ve contributed so much of their lives. Individual volunteers, families and groups will be welcome. Some of the opportunities availible are:
  • Activity Assistant
  • Music Therapy Group
  • Songs for Seniors/iPod Program
  • Horticultural Therapy
  • Resident Visitation
  • Outings Escort
